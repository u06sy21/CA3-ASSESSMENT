Chengdu is very old in city in China,the history is more than 2000 years, maybe even longer. In Chengdu, there is a museum called "the Jinsha Site Museum",this museum record the history of the people lived in Chengdu area at 3500 years ago, not just record their life but also include their culture and bronzewares.

Chengdu is also a capital city, especially in the Three Kingdoms Period (220-280 year),Chengdu was the captial of Kingdom "Shu", and there is a buliding called "Temple of Marquis Wu Zhuge Liang" which bulid in "Jin" dynasty to memorialize Zhuge Liang who is the the prime minister of the "Shu" Kingdom. And people always memorialize him until today.Except him, the temple also introduced the stories for the Kingdom generals, and sing them for the deeds of defending the country and protect the man in the street.
