In the Map of Chengdu, you will see there are two locations with a camera logal, they are very famous in Chengdu.

The Du Fu's thatched cottage

The Du Fu's cottage is one of famous ancient buliding in Chengdu,it was the former residence of the poet's retreat in Tang dynasty who is Du Fu. You can enjoy the scenery at here and understand some history about him.

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding

One of the special in Chengdu is giant panda, the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding is the best place for you to look the giant panda. You can also learn some interesting knowledge about giant panda, and stories of protection of giant panda.

By the way, wxcept these two place, there are more good place for you to visit in Chengdu such as "the Jinsha Site Museum", except this museum, you can also have a nice tea time in any teahouses in Chengdu which is a very popular pastime in locals.
